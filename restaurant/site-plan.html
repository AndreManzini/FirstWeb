Overview

Purpose

The site has a goal to share information about the restaurant to attract more people, and increase revenue. Also, we will display the food available to let customers know what they should expect. We want people to be excited to come to the restaurant and have a good time eating.

Audience

Our audience is people that love to eat out or that does not have time to cook meals. The group age we will aim for is something between 16 to 75 years old. We expect those people to like Italian food.

About us

Philosophy

La nostr famiglia's philosophy is about the pleasure of enjoying a gathering around the table to share traditional Italian dishes, whether for a casual dinner, an indulgent lunch, or a celebratory party. La nostr famiglia is a place where you can show up and be welcomed. It doesn't matter whether you arrive early or late, smart or casual, at La nostr famiglia you'll be treated like a friend. Our goal is to combine the warmth and genuine hospitality of a classic Italian trattoria with our passion for sourcing the best organic and imported ingredients.

Who is Chef Andre Mori?

I grew up in Araraquara Brasil, not far from Venice. My family is a mix of Italian and Brazilian people. From a very young age, I had the chance to learn about the two different kinds of cuisine that come from these areas, and I also learned about the ingredients and ways of cooking from both regions. I have always had a passion for cooking. I remember trying to help my mother in the kitchen when I was very young. I attended a college in the city of Sao Paulo where I got a degree specializing in Italian cuisine. I like cooking everything, and I always treat the ingredients I use with a lot of respect. For me, the most important thing is making my customers happy.
